Simon Game

Press any key to start game

Total Points:0

Highest Points:0

Objective

Memorize and repeat the exact sequence of flashing color tiles. The sequence grows by one tile every round.

Controls

  • Press Any Key to start or restart.
  • Click the color tiles to input the sequence.

How to Play

  1. Press Any Key to begin.
  2. Watch the tiles light up in order.
  3. Click the tiles in the same order.
  4. Complete the round to earn a point and see a longer sequence next round.

Scoring

  • +10 point for every round completed.
  • Total Points: your current run.
  • Highest Points: your best score so far (persists until page refresh).

Game Over

If you click the wrong tile at any time, the game ends immediately.

Restart: Press Any Key to try again. Your highest score remains.

Tips

  • Break long sequences into small chunks.
  • Say the color names in your head to build rhythm.
  • Stay calm—the pace can feel faster as sequences get longer.
